Don’t wait, renew today!

You should have recently received a letter with detailed information about
how to renew your 2016 health coverage.

1 Review your 2016 plan to be sure it fits your needs
2 Make sure that your Advanced Premium Tax Credit (APTC) amount is correct
3 Submit changes by December 15, 2015*

To confirm your 2016 APTC, log in to your Marketplace Account on www.healthcare.gov,
use your same username and password from last year, and update your application.
