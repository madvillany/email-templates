We're expanding our service area

We’re excited to announce Aetna Whole Health – Banner Health Network plans are now available to more members and plan sponsors in Pinal County. We’ve expanded our service area to include all of Pinal County, including the Casa Grande area.

What you should know

• For new or renewing business, plans are available effective November 1, 2015
• Members currently enrolled in an Aetna Whole Health – Banner Health Network plan can take advantage of the expansion now. Providers in the expanded service area can be found using DocFind®
• Individual plans are not included in the service area expansion at this time

A new way of looking at health care

When Aetna and Banner Health Network team up, you can save big on your health care premiums. That’s because we’ve designed Aetna Whole Health to:

• Reward doctors for improving patient care quality
• Lower medical cost growth over time by reducing waste, improving care coordination and closing care gaps
• Enhance each patient’s experience with innovative care management programs
• Support effective patient and primary care doctor relationships

That’s member-focused, doctor-driven health care from Banner Health Network.
