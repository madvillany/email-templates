About Lloyd David

Lloyd David, MBA Executive Director/CEO

 

Lloyd David is the Chief Executive Officer/Executive Director of The Polyclinic, a multi-specialty group located on First Hill in Seattle. The Polyclinic has 237 health care providers, including 191 physicians with approximately 40 percent primary care (including family medicine and pediatrics) and 60 percent medical and surgical specialists. The Polyclinic has 15 locations and serves 235,000 patients. The clinic provides a wide range of diagnostic services in house, including ambulatory surgery, full-service radiology, cardiology diagnostics, etc. The Polyclinic has Medicare and full-risk capitation contracts.

Mr. David currently serves on the Seattle Chamber Board of Advisors and on the Physicians Insurance Board of Directors. He helped found the Washington Health Alliance, dedicated to improving health care quality and cost in the region. He previously served as president of the Washington Medical Group Alliance, an association of large medical groups across the state. Prior to joining The Polyclinic in 1994, Mr. David served in a variety of administrative roles at Harborview Medical Center, where his responsibilities included strategic and facility planning, marketing, community relations, regulatory affairs, funding development and organization training and development. He holds a BA in Government from Harvard University and an MBA from the University of Washington.
